There are different ways to use the implemented smart-home technology in your home. Either it is with your phone, letting the house act on it's own or even just talking to it!

"Alexa, turn on bedside lights" — "..turn on main light" — "Alexa, open the curtains". Have you ever dream on talking to your home? sounds crazy! but it is now possible. (works with Google Home too)
